Welcome to City Care & Support

At City Care and Support, we specialise in providing compassionate support for individuals
facing various challenges, including mental health issues, forensic mental health, complex needs,
domestic violence, homelessness, and substance dependency. We are proud to announce that we are
expanding our services to include support for Autism, ADHD, and both high and low needs in learning difficulties.

Our Services

We support individuals through services that foster stability, resilience, and personal growth.

Supported Accommodation

Supported Accommodation

We provide safe, high-quality housing for individuals who need support due to mental health challenges, learning disabilities, or other vulnerabilities. Our accommodations are fully managed, ensuring that residents live in a secure and structured environment.Our trained staff provide continuous support, assisting residents with daily activities and life skills. We work closely with local councils and support agencies to ensure each individual receives the best possible care. Our goal is to help residents transition smoothly into independent living when they are ready.

Mental Health & Well-Being Support

Mental Health & Well-Being Support

We understand the importance of mental health and provide specialized support for individuals experiencing emotional and psychological challenges. Our trained professionals offer one-on-one or two-on-one support, helping residents manage anxiety, depression, and other mental health conditions. We encourage participation in therapy sessions, peer support groups, and structured activities that promote emotional well-being. Our team works alongside healthcare providers to ensure residents receive the appropriate medical and therapeutic care.

Independent Living

Independent Living

We prepare individuals for independent living by equipping them with essential life skills and providing step-by-step guidance. Our team assists with budgeting, meal planning, household management to ensure a smooth transition. We encourage residents to take responsibility for their daily routines while offering the necessary support when needed. We also provide training on tenancy management, helping individuals understand their rights and responsibilities as tenants. We work with local housing authorities and support networks to provide continuous assistance even after individuals transition to independent living.

Financial & Housing

Financial & Housing

Navigating financial and housing matters can be overwhelming, especially for individuals with complex needs. We provide guidance on securing housing benefits, managing rent payments, and applying for financial assistance. Our team helps residents understand their entitlements and ensures they receive the support they are eligible for. We assist with completing housing benefit and council tax support/exemption applications and liaise with local councils to resolve any issues. Our goal is to prevent homelessness and financial distress by providing residents with the knowledge and tools to maintain stable housing.

Daily Living Assistance

Daily Living Assistance

We offer personalized support to help residents manage their day-to-day activities with confidence. Our services include support with hygiene, dressing, and grooming, ensuring individuals maintain their dignity and well-being. We help with medication reminders and management to ensure residents follow their prescribed treatments. We also assist with mobility support and encourage participation in social activities to prevent isolation. Our focus is on promoting independence while ensuring that residents receive the necessary care to maintain their quality of life.
